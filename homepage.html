Uber

Uber is a US multinational technology company on the Internet, based in San Francisco, California.
It has developed markets that operate on the Uber mobile application, which allows smart phone users to request
a driver with his car for the purpose of tranportation, and drivers are also users of the same application but to benefit
from it knowing that Uber deducts fees for using the application from drivers on every trip. Uber is allowed in 900 cities around the world. In addition to giving riders a way to get from point A to point B, we're working to bring the future closer
with self-driving technology and urban air transport, helping people order food quickly and affordably, removing
barriers to healthcare, creating new freight-booking solutions, and helping companies provide a seamless employee
travel experience.
